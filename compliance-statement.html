HIPAA Compliance

Committed to safeguarding the privacy, security, and integrity of Protected Health Information (PHI).

01 Our Commitment

At Zenexa Infotech Pvt Ltd, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy, security, and integrity of Protected Health Information (PHI) in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA) and applicable regulations.

  • Privacy Rule: We limit the collection, use, and disclosure of PHI to what is permitted under HIPAA.
  • Security Rule: We employ administrative, physical, and technical safeguards including encryption and access controls.
  • Breach Notification: We have strict protocols for investigating and notifying of any data breaches.
  • BAAs: We enter into Business Associate Agreements with all covered entities.

02 Safeguards in Place

Encryption

All PHI is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Access Control

Role-based access restricted to authorized personnel.

Training

Regular HIPAA and data protection training for all staff.

Monitoring

24/7 system monitoring and audit logs.

03 Your Rights

Individuals whose PHI we handle retain rights under HIPAA, including the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of their health information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete records
  • Request restrictions on certain uses or disclosures
  • File a complaint if rights have been violated
